Magic Kingdom

Magic Kingdom park is known for its iconic Cinderella Castle. This park is split into six themed lands, which are Main Street USA, Adventure Land, Frontier Land, Fantasy Land, Tomorrow Land, and Liberty Square. Its slogan is "The most magical place on earth".
