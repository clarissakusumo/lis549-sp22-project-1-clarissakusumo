Hollywood Studios

Hollywood Studios park is known for its iconic Tower of Terror building. This park is split into six themed lands, which are Hollywood Boulevard, Echo Lake, Grand Avenue, Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge, Toy Story Land, Animation Courtyard, and Sunset Boulevard. Its slogan is "Imagined worls of Hollywood". If you are looking for thrill attractions, you'd need to make this theme park your priority! Hollywood Studios is the most crowded theme parks due to the popular attractions that it offers, and it's also the smallest theme park (size wise) compared to Magic Kingdom, Epcot, and Animal Kingdom.
