Magic Kingdom

1. Dole Whip

Dole whip is a very popular disneyworld's snacks that you can get in Magic Kingdom. It's usually a combination of pineapple juice + pineapple ice cream. They also have more variations to dole whip. You can get dole whip from Aloha Isle.

2. Cheeseburger Spring Roll

You can get this from Spring Rolls cart in front of the main Adventureland entrance.

3. Mini Corn Dogs

You can get this from Casey's Corner

4. Fruit and Nutella Waffle Sandwich

You can get this from Sleepy Hollow Inn (before 5pm)

Hollywood Studios

1. Ice Cream Cookie Sandwich

You can find this snack at Hollywood Scoops which is right before the entrance of Tower of Terror.

2. Ronto Wrap

You can get this from Ronto Roasters inside Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge

3. Totchos

You can get this from Woody's Lunch Box

Epcot

1. Churro Bites with Caramel Sauce

You can get this from La Cantina de San Angel in the Mexico Pavilion.

2. Nutella Filled Beignets

You can get this from Les Halles Boulangerie-Patisserie in France.

3. Kaki Gori

You can get this from Kabuki Cafe in Japan.

4. School Bread

You can get this from Kringla Bakeri Og Kafe in Norway

Animal Kingdom

1. Mr. Kamal's Fries

You can get this from Mr Kamal's in between Africa and Asia.

2. Cheeseburger Steamed Pods

You can get this from Satu'li Canteen inside Pandora.

3. Pork Egg Rolls

You can get this from Yak and Yeti Local Food Cafes.

4. Malva Cake Sundae

You can get this from Tamu Tamu Refreshments in Africa.
